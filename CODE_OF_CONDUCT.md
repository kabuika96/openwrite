# Code of Conduct

OpenWrite contributors are expected to keep project discussion focused, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss code, design, and tradeoffs directly without personal attacks.
- Assume contributors are working in good faith unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Keep bug reports and pull request reviews specific enough to act on.
- Do not post private vault content, credentials, personal data, or machine-specific secrets.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory comments.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Publishing private information without consent.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and close discussions that violate these expectations.
